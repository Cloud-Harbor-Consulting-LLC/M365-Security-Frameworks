## Format rules for this template

- Half page to 1 page maximum. If it does not fit, cut something.
- High severity material incidents only. Do not include Medium or below.
- No alert volume metrics. No raw counts of blocked phishing emails or failed logins.
- Every number on this page should tie to a decision the board can make, or to a trend that informs the next investment cycle.
